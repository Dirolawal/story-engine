## Strategic Filters (Apply To Every Carousel)

- **Use "career builder" framing, NOT "escape the 9-5."** The latter pulls in tyre-kickers without savings or commitment. Sales as a long-term skill set, not a quick exit.
- **Hooks must target the sales-curious or athletic-disciplined 23-year-old**, not "anyone who hates their 9-5."
- **CTA must filter hard** — "DM HAVEN if you're serious" not "DM me if interested." Rotate HAVEN / FIRE / STORY as trigger words.
- **Student transformation slides emphasise the WORK**, not just the result.
- **Freedom hook still works** but pair it with specific identity language (athlete, ex-corporate, ex-9-5er), not generic.
